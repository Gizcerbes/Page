Deleting your account and related data.

  1. To delete an account, go to the application settings and click the delete account button.
  2. The data will be deleted automatically after 7 days.
  3. Uninstall the application.

We don't collect data that can be attributed to a specific user unless the user is signed in with a Google account, and we can't delete what we don't have.

For users without authorization, the data is stored locally on the device and to delete it, simply clear the application data or delete the application from your device.

If for some reason you cannot enter the application.

  1. Send a deletion request to gizserbes@gmail.com. The message must be sent from the email address used to register for the application.
  2. The moderator will activate automatic deletion and your account will be deleted after 7 days.
  3. Uninstall the application.

Deletion can be canceled within 7 days.
